Humberstones Jewellers have been selling traditional watches and jewellery with brands such as Rolex, Audemars Piguet and Tag Heuer for over 50 years. With th extensive growth in the online market they have begun a digital transformation. This has envolved a redesign of there website to improve the user experience of purchasing products online and increase their online presence.

I was brought in as a user experience and visual designer to help with the website redesign. This envolved me understanding the types of customers were coming to their site and what were the main drop off points. By using a series of analytics we were able to establish the main reason for drop offs. Users were leaving the site due to poor navigation, as users were trying to serach for certain products but were not able to find them.

With this and more useful insights I began to mock up concepts and continued to play them back to the client. The designs below are the current concepts for key pages. This project is still in process with further developement still to come.
