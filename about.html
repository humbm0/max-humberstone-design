I am a Designer who specializes in user centred design, user research, user experience and visual design. – I work in UI, UX and in Web Design as well as intergration of industrial design products to enhance digital experiences. I produce handmade HTML5, CSS3 & Responsive code.

I work with people and companies to define & develop products, to make sure that the products they produce perform and succeed to meet user needs and business goals. To do so I dedicate myself to each and every brief, designing visual interfaces and experiences.

I always put quality first.

Early experience working with global teams within a number of different industries including; Energy & Utilities, Financial Services, Consumer Goods, Telecomunications and the Public Sector has given me great insight into different user needs and business goals.

My greatest strength is the ability to rapidly ideate develop and test creative solutions with attention to detail. This is possible with extensive knoledge in prototyping tools to quickly develop, high-fidelity concepts and test with real users early in the design process to ensure the product meets user needs.

Always looking for new, interesting projects to work on. If you like my work make sure you get in contact and lets create amazing digital experience.

User Experience

User experience is the core of my design process. I use design thinking to gain understanding and empathy with the user which allows me to establish needs, generate ideas and test continuously to produce products that exceed expectations and bring memorable experiences.

Visual

With a passion for aesthetics in both physical and digital I study and learn brand identities to push the boundaries beyond just static visual design and develop interfaces that respond and interact with sense and purpose to the user's intent.

Product

Studying Industrial Design taught me the foundation in understanding user needs in order to solve problems. This has led me to intergrate digital design with physical products to create products that have multiple touch points and intergrate with the modern world.

Development

With knolledge in html, css and javascript I can produce high fidelity prototypes, responsive websites and hybrid apps. This has also allowed me to develop physical prototypes using Arduinos to test functional concepts with real users early on in the design process.

Experience

Early experience working within a range of industries and clients has allowed me to develop skills in many practices which I continue to use in my work.
