Completed

Severn Trent are constanlty pushing to help their customers in ways that exceed expectation. However, during Feburary 2016 Severn Trent experienced problems with their water supply and as a result some customers do not recieve the help they needed quickly because they were not registered to Severn Trent as customer who needs extra help.

Severn Trench approached us to design a web app for their customers to register themselves as a priority customer. This was fast paced project to design and build the web app in two weeks which could then be released to small number of real customer to test and provide feedback on the service.

THE FOCUS

We received valuable user research from the customer service team at Severn Trent which used to drive our user needs and design direction.

The current process of registering to the priority service envolves a user calling Sevren Trent and speaking with call center staff. This can be difficult for some user due to missing call times. We therefor decided an interactive online form would be a good solution as it would be accessible 24/7 and could be designed to simplify the information presented to the user at each point.

One of the main reasons why some customers were not regustered with Severn Trent was that they did not realise they were eligiable for the priority service or were not aware the benifits of the service. This led us to look at how might we simplify the process of checking a users eligiabilty.

Our expectations were absolutely surpassed. We are so glad we chose Woodbury for our new project!
