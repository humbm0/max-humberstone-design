SHORTLISTED FOR PRESENTATION AT O2

Brief set together with the O2 Customer Experience Design and Connected Home teams.

"Design an innovative Interactive Connected Home concept that enhances the experience of home for a clearly identified group of users."

Day to day activities of students and young adults can be difficult to keep track of. Balancing work and a relationships with distant family members can present a common challenge to many people.

OBSERVE

Mixture of fly on the wall, shadowing and contextual enquiry research with student and parents with the aim to establish the as-is situation and user needs.

REFLECT

From the user research we were able to gain an understanding of the situation, the purpose, and how I can make a difference for the users.

USER NEEDS

Know when the other user is free to socialise

A more intimate way to connect when a face to face meet is not available

Help to organise day to day and fit in more time to connect with family and friends

Enlarge
MAKE & TEST

Generation of idea cards to explore possible solutions that meet the user needs. Ideas are then developed into initial wireframes which are tested with users to validate the concepts. Further feedback is taken and help to develop the wireframes further into higher fidelity screens.

Enlarge

“I love talking to my parents or grandparents but they can sometimes call me at bad time when I might be really busy with work and not available to talk.”

By speaking with students and parents I was able to identify new opportunities, evaluate existing ideas and figure out exactly who I was aiming to serve and what’s important to them.

By asking open-ended questions I could absorb the things I was told without judgement.

THE SOLUTION

O2 Connect is an application which uses the smart home concept with a twist. By using a combination of connected devices in the home, personal/social calendars on smart phones and GPS location, O2 Connect learns the users routine to suggest the best time to call, video chat or message with family and friends. O2 Connect will also detect changes in schedule and proactively suggest new times based on the situation.

GPS location, selected personal and social media calendars, and connectivity of devices to create a smart home ecosystem that tracks and learns a users routine. This is then used to determine the best time for all users to connect with each other.

The intergration of cloud calendars provides a platform for photo sharing directly into the family home via the use of electronic photo frames, smart TVs and other connect devices.

New video chat UI design creates a more imersive experience by allowing users to connect with smart devices in their home and display the chat on multiple screens.

Innovative calendar redesign that aims to simplify the information presented to the user and easy to understand when calls have been scheduled.

KEEPS UP WITH YOUR BUSY LIVES

Using GPS and connect home devices O2 Connect will detect changes in a users schedule and remind the user of their scheduled chat or suggest new times.
