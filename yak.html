From interviews with kayak users we established that cooking over a fire as a group was part of the experince and the new products that focused on freeze-dried or packaged food did not appeal to them

They also said that they would tend to eat snacks such as chocolate or sweets during the day and therefore did not get the nutritional value they needed while doing a high energy activity. This was down to how long it takes to prepare to cook meals and they would waste to much time if they were to cook lunch.

THE FOCUS

Design a solution that allows users to cook as a group but reduce the time spent to prepare and therefore ensures users get the nutritional value they need.

The product must also not take up more space than the conventional cooking equiptment inside the kayak.

I know I don't eat as much as I should but it just take too long to cook three meals. I'd never reach the end point!

THE SOLUTION

Yak is new tool for kayakers that uses a new self heating technology that does not require the need to mix hazardous chemicals near food. The reaction of aluminium and silica is a dry mixture that when activated generates heat in around 2 minutes much faster than any other self heating technology. These fuels also provide a far greater food : heat ratio meaning more room for those vital calories.

Yak also allows users to prepare a head of time and store two types of food, lunch and dinner for example. This ensures users can obtain the high number of calories they need when out kayaking.

Yak is designed not just to provide hot food for kayakers but to reduce cooking time and insure users get the nutritional values they need to keep them on the water for longer.

FEATURES

Two identical containers attached via a connector or divider. Using the connector user can carry 1200ml of one type of food. When using the divider the user can carry 600ml of two different types of food.

Dual lid to consume drinks or sol- id food. Both spring loaded to give resistive feedback to users when closing.

Spring loaded ergonomic release mechanism for fast quick access to food. Ability to open if wearing gloves.

Different heat capsules available designed for different food types. Precisely calibrated based on the mix of the fuel in the HeatGenie heater. Specific properties for a given food or beverage impacts its heating characteristics.

ENHANCE THE COMPLETE JOURNEY

Yak is a product that aims to enhance the whole experience of kayaking and therefore an app was designed to allow users to download and share routes with other users. Yak will then work out the best places to stop and notify the user when they have reached their rest point to ensure users get the nutritional values they need for each trip.

It also allows them to order heating capsules when they are running low and suggests meals for when out on the water.
