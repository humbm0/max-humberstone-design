Design a desirable product that allows hot food to be eaten on location. Consider the product’s main role as a way to create, enhance or extend an interesting and desirable experience rather than just an outdoor eating device.

From interviews with kayak users we established that cooking over a fire as a group was part of the experince and the new products that focused on freeze-dried or packaged food did not appeal to them

They also said that they would tend to eat snacks such as chocolate or sweets during the day and therefore did not get the nutritional value they needed while doing a high energy activity. This was down to how long it takes to prepare to cook meals and they would waste to much time if they were to cook lunch.

THE FOCUS

Design a solution that allows users to cook as a group but reduce the time spent to prepare and therefore ensures users get the nutritional value they need.

The product must also not take up more space than the conventional cooking equiptment inside the kayak.

I know I don't eat as much as I should but it just take too long to cook three meals. I'd never reach the end point!

THE SOLUTION

O2 Connect is an application that takes the smart home concept with a twist. By using a combination of connected devices in the home, personal/social calendars on smart phones and GPS location, O2 Connect learns the users routine to suggest the best time to call, video chat or message with family and friends. O2 Connect will also detect changes in schedule and proactively suggest new times based on the situation.

The intergration of cloud calendars provides a platform for photo sharing directly into the family home via the use of electronic photo frames, smart TVs and other connect devices.

New video chat UI design that creates a more imersive experince by allowing users to point there smart at conneted smart TVs and drag users onto the screen.

Innovative calendar redesign that aims to simplify the information presented to the user and easy to understand when calls have been scheduled.
