LDS Degree Show App

A self tasked project to enhance the digital experience of the Loughborough Design School Degree Show.

Students only have a very small window to get their work spotted during the degree show so I wanted to give design students more of a pressence during the short time the Degree is on. I also wanted to include some social aspects to the app, to allow user to get a good understanding of which projects are hot.

This has led me to look at allowing users to like projects and then view projects and designers ordered by likes, sector and which course they studying.

The degree show is all about showing off their hard work and hope that proffesionals will get in touch with possible job offers. So by having the students work stored in the pockets of the proffesions, along with their contact details this will hopefully help students be remebered.

The application is still in development but will be released on the Apple App Store and Google Play before June 2017 ready for the degree show.
