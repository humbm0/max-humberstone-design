This project was developed as part of the Global Design Jam 2016. As a team of 6 we were given 48 hours to design a product service based on a given brief.

The brief given was the sound of a water drop. From here, as individuals, we had 5 minutes to ideate and then pitch to piers and decide on the direction the team would head.

Based on similar ideas around helping people with disabilities we decided to concentrate on mental health as a disability and how might we provide a service to help young people with mental health issues.

UNDERSTAND

One of our team members told us that she had suffered with mental illness and was happy to talk with us about the problems she suffered. This allowed us to gain invaluable insights into the struggles young people with mental health issues have.

We were also able to speak with a member of the team friend who had suffered with a mental illness and was happy to have a phone interview. She was also happy to become our sponsoured user who we kept in touch with throughout the two days playing back our ideas and concepts to her to enure we were meeting the user needs and solving the problems we set out to.

INSIGHTS

From the inetrviews and discussions with users we were able to establish the key problem insights.

The cause of users going into a “downward spiral” are triggers that users will experince. This then causes them to focus on negative thoughts and close off to other people.

There is a serious lack in existing help such as meeting groups and are very irregular. If a user requires proffesional help from the NHS or other health organisations, this can take a long period of time and there allow a user to enter a “downward spiral”.

Talking with people who also suffer with mental health can be useful but in a controlled enviroment. Web forums allow users to talk about about negative thoughts and make the situation worse.

THE FOCUS

How might we proactively prevent a user from entering a “downward spiral“ by providing an immeadiate distraction upon a user’s trigger.

Provide an immeadiate distraction

Encourage positive thoughts

Help from people other mental health sufferers but prevent unhealthy discussions that occur on web forums

Enlarge
THE SOLUTION

Feals is a smart phone application that provides people with mental health problems distraction to prevent them from a downward spiral and encourage positive thinking.

Learns the types of triggers and predicts future triggers based on journal entries.

Learns what types of activitives work best to distract you based on journal entries.

Find friends to help you during triggers based on theropies and interests.

Emergency contact number in the event of an incident.

KEEPS UP WITH YOUR BUSY LIVES

Using GPS and connect home devices O2 Connect will detect changes in a users schedule and remind the user of their scheduled chat or suggest new times.
